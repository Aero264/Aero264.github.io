Academic Appointments #

Research Fellow in Mathematical Modelling and Model–Data Fusion
University of Surrey · May 2024 – present

  • Principal Investigator on the UK Dementia Research Institute – LifeArc pilot award, “A computational biomarker to screen for epilepsy in Alzheimer’s disease”.
  • Designing and conducting overnight human sleep EEG studies.
  • Developing real-time EEG phase estimation and closed-loop auditory stimulation.
  • Built and validated a comprehensive EEG analysis pipeline (artefact handling, spectral analysis, connectivity, event detection) across multiple cohorts.
  • Model-driven interpretation of sleep and circadian influences in ageing and neurodegeneration.

Research Fellow in Computational Neuroscience
University of Birmingham · October 2021 – May 2024

  • Co-Investigator on the EPSRC-funded ATMOSPHERE project for seizure forecasting using wearable physiological data, working closely with clinicians and computer scientists.
  • Developed mechanistic models of seizure onset and termination, framing spread of epileptic activity as an epidemic-like process.

Research Associate in Aerospace Engineering
University of Bristol · September 2019 – September 2021

  • Developed a multibody dynamics framework for rotorcraft systems in MATLAB.
  • Integrated object-oriented modelling and automatic differentiation to automate derivation and simulation of rotorcraft dynamics.

Visiting Researcher
Swansea University · 2017, 2019

  • Developed LQR-based state-feedback control strategies to stabilise chaotic oscillations in nonlinear structures.
